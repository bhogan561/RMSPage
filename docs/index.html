How It Works

Saved by the Sensor

Early Detection Prevents a Major Category 3 Sewage Loss

  1. The Threat: After-hours cleaners started filling a mop bucket at a utility sink, then stepped away as a backed-up drain line pushed raw sewage into the sink until it overflowed.
  2. The RMS Response: Shortly after Category 3 water reached the floor, a strategically placed RMS water sensor detected the intrusion and sent instant alerts to the RMS Response Team and property management.
  3. The Action: An RMS responder arrived within minutes, confirmed the sewage backup, shut off the water source, stopped the spread, and began targeted cleanup, sanitizing, and drying.
  4. The Impact: The response contained the sewage/water-loss risk before contaminated water reached adjacent tenant spaces, avoiding demolition, material removal, tenant displacement, and a broader restoration scope.

About Ready Monitoring Services

Ready Monitoring Services (RMS) helps commercial property owners detect problems before they turn into expensive disasters. Our wireless sensors monitor for water leaks, temperature changes, humidity, power loss, and other critical conditions 24/7. When an issue is detected, the system immediately sends alerts by text, email, and mobile notification while displaying live information on an easy-to-use dashboard, giving your team the opportunity to respond in minutes instead of discovering a problem hours or days later.

RMS is powered by Property Restoration of the Capital Region, and that's what sets us apart. We've responded to enough of these losses to know that the biggest factor in reducing damage is finding the problem early. We also know that most property owners don't have maintenance staff on-site 24 hours a day, and even the best teams can't be everywhere at once.

That's why we built Ready Monitoring Services. It's a practical solution designed from real restoration experience to give property owners another set of eyes on their buildings around the clock.
